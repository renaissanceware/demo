Best Practices

Category Creation Tips

  • Use clear, descriptive names for your categories
  • Keep category names short and memorable
  • Consider grouping similar expenses together
  • Add descriptions to provide context
  • Use icons to make categories visually distinctive

Icon Guidelines

  • Use square images (recommended size: 24x24 pixels)
  • Choose icons that clearly represent the category
  • Use transparent backgrounds for better appearance
  • Ensure icons are from trusted sources

Common Categories

  • Food & Dining - Restaurants, groceries, coffee
  • Transportation - Gas, public transit, rideshare
  • Shopping - Clothing, electronics, household items
  • Entertainment - Movies, games, subscriptions
  • Bills & Utilities - Rent, electricity, internet
  • Healthcare - Doctor visits, medications
  • Education - Courses, books, supplies

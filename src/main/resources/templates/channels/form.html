Best Practices

Channel Creation Tips

  • Use clear, descriptive names for your channels
  • Keep channel names short and memorable
  • Add descriptions to provide context
  • Use icons to make channels visually distinctive

Icon Guidelines

  • Use square images (recommended size: 24x24 pixels)
  • Choose icons that clearly represent the channel
  • Use transparent backgrounds for better appearance
  • Ensure icons are from trusted sources

Common Channels

  • Email - Email communication
  • Phone - Phone calls and messages
  • Website - Website visitors
  • Social Media - Social media platforms
  • Referral - Referral sources
  • Direct - Direct traffic

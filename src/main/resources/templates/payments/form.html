Best Practices

Payment Provider Creation Tips

  • Use clear, descriptive names for your payment providers
  • Keep payment provider names short and memorable
  • Consider grouping similar payment providers together
  • Add descriptions to provide context
  • Use icons to make payment providers visually distinctive

CSS Class Guidelines

  • Use Bootstrap Icons or other icon library classes
  • Choose classes that clearly represent the payment provider
  • Example: bi-credit-card, bi-cash, bi-bank
  • Ensure the icon library is properly loaded in the application

Common Payment Providers

  • Cash - Physical currency
  • Credit Card - Visa, Mastercard, American Express
  • Debit Card - Direct bank account payment
  • Digital Wallet - PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay
  • Bank Transfer - Direct bank-to-bank transfer
  • Check - Paper check payment
  • Mobile Payment - WeChat Pay, Alipay

Best Practices

Expense Entry Tips

  • Use clear, descriptive titles for your expenses
  • Always include the exact amount
  • Select the most appropriate category
  • Enter the correct date when the expense occurred
  • Add notes for additional context
  • Mark as confirmed once verified

Organization Tips

  • Record expenses as soon as possible
  • Use consistent naming conventions
  • Review and categorize regularly
  • Use channels to track how you learned about the expense

Common Expenses

  • Food & Dining - Restaurants, groceries, coffee
  • Transportation - Fuel, public transit, rideshare
  • Housing - Rent, mortgage, utilities
  • Entertainment - Movies, games, events
  • Shopping - Clothing, electronics, household items
  • Healthcare - Doctor visits, medications, insurance
